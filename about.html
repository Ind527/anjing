About Us

Learn more about Golden Bean — your trusted partner in waterproof coatings

Who We Are

Golden Bean (Pangugreen) is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-performance waterproof coatings, metallic paints, tile paints, and specialty construction materials.

Founded with a mission to deliver durable, eco-friendly, and cost-effective solutions, we serve homeowners, contractors, and construction professionals across the globe.

Our products are engineered to withstand the harshest conditions — from extreme temperatures and heavy rainfall to high-pressure environments — ensuring lasting protection for every surface.

Our Mission

We are committed to innovating sustainable building solutions that protect homes, commercial properties, and infrastructure — while minimizing environmental impact.

Every product we manufacture goes through rigorous quality testing to meet international standards, ensuring you get performance you can rely on.

Our state-of-the-art production facilities and experienced R&D team continuously develop new formulations to address the evolving needs of the construction industry.
