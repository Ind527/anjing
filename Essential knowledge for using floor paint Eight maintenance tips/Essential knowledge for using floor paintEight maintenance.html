Essential knowledge for using floor paint: Eight maintenance tips

  • April 8, 2025
  • Posted by 18067044270@163.com
08 Apr

With the improvement of quality of life, people’s requirements for living environment are becoming increasingly strict, therefore, the use of floor paint is becoming more and more widespread. Next, we will explore several key points to pay attention to when using floor paint:

  1. In the environment where production and construction are carried out simultaneously, it is necessary to ensure that the construction area is fully isolated to prevent impurities such as dust and sand from entering the epoxy floor paint film that has not been completely dried;
  2. For areas that are easily contaminated or conspicuous, such as storefronts and corridors, a wide cloth mop should be used for cleaning at least 1-2 times a day;
  3. Once coarse and hard sand particles or mud are found on the ground, they should be cleaned immediately to reduce the wear of sand particles on the ground;
  4. Employees need to switch to soft soled shoes before entering the clean area; At the junction or entrance where sand particles and mud are easily carried in, plastic blankets should be installed so that employees can remove debris from their shoe soles when entering or exiting.
  5. When unloading with forklifts and forklifts, it is important to ensure that the goods are placed smoothly to prevent them from tipping or dragging and scratching the epoxy floor;
  6. Vehicles, forklifts, and forklifts should minimize braking during operation to reduce unnecessary wear and tear. For areas that frequently leak oil, it is necessary to regularly clean them with sawdust to prevent the oil from deteriorating and adsorbing dust, forming difficult to remove stains. In areas with large areas of oil immersion, a wide cloth mop can be used to push sawdust to absorb the oil on the floor;
  7. Be careful and cautious when using machine repair tools to avoid them falling to the ground. Once significant damage is found on the paint film, immediate repair should be reported to ensure timely repair before oil stains penetrate the foundation;
  8. When washing the floor with water in the workshop, attention should be paid to avoiding the accumulation of oil-water mixture in deeply damaged areas to prevent difficulty in removal.
    The above are eight key points to pay attention to when using floor paint.
