Some words about us

We Help Everyone Enjoy Amazing Products

Golden Bean Paint Technology Co., Ltd. is a self-produced and self-sold high-tech enterprise deeply rooted in China's coating market. With its full-industry-chain layout and strict quality control, it has established a solid reputation in the industry.

Advanced Production and Technological StrengthThe company has invested heavily in introducing cutting-edge international automated production equipment. From raw material proportioning, mixing and emulsification to finished product filling, the entire process realizes intelligent control. This not only greatly improves production efficiency but also ensures the performance stability of each batch of products. Meanwhile, relying on an R&D team composed of senior engineers and technical experts, the company always focuses on the forefront of industry technology, continuously iterates formulas and production processes. Its core technical indicators have consistently ranked among the top in the industry, enabling it to accurately meet the diverse coating needs in different scenarios.

At present, the company mainly produces and operates products including floor paint series, interior wall paint series, interior wall art paint series, cultural stone series, exterior wall paint series, and water-based industrial paint series.

Golden Bean Paint Technology Co., Ltd. was established on January 8, 2020, with a registered address at No. 12 Xingmei Avenue, Xinchang County. It is an industrial and trade company that focuses on the research and development, production, and sales of water-based environmentally friendly coatings. Committed to providing efficient and responsive zero inventory solutions for dealers and agents. At present, a nationwide express and logistics supply chain has been formed, ensuring the stable progress of the goods and sales work of agents and distributors.
